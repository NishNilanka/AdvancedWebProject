CloudResume

Host your resume on the cloud

Objectives

Create your resume and share it with the interested parties. They can download it, share it and bookmark it. You can see how many times your resume has been watched, downloaded and bookmarked.

Profile

A Software-as-a-Service solution created with Ruby on Rails, and hosted on 'Heroku Clouds'.

Register

Register simply with providing your email and a password. Login with social networks like Facebook, twitter Google+ is on the way!
